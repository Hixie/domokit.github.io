MultiTapGestureRecognizer

MultiTapGestureRecognizer is a tap recognizer that treats taps independently. That is, each pointer sequence that could resolve to a tap does so independently of others: down-1, down-2, up-1, up-2 produces two taps, on up-1 and up-2.

TapGestureRecognizer

TapGestureRecognizer is a tap recognizer that tracks only one primary pointer per gesture. That is, during tap recognition, extra pointer events are ignored: down-1, down-2, up-1, up-2 produces only one tap on up-1.
